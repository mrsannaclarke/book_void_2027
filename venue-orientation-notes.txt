VOID takes over two connected parts of the Jupiter property: the 2nd floor of Jupiter NEXT and nearly all of Jupiter Original.

Jupiter NEXT:
- Only the 2nd floor is part of the convention.
- Hey Love is the bar inside the Jupiter NEXT building.

Jupiter Original / OG:
- The convention uses essentially the entire two-story OG property, indoor and outdoor.
- The Dream Tent sits inside the OG footprint, around/over the area where 1-6 and A-G are.
- Some OG rooms are converted into tattoo booths, while other rooms remain overnight hotel rooms.

Not part of the convention footprint:
- Doug Fir
- KBOO

Suggested attendee-facing wording:
VOID takes over two connected parts of the Jupiter property: the 2nd floor of Jupiter NEXT and nearly all of Jupiter Original.
